Welcome

Welcome to Linux-4-Kids! Our goal is to provide you with easy access to a resource to learn about Linux and it's terminal. Our tutorials are aimed at younger a younger audience with little to no background with Linux. By using our tutorials you will learn what Linux is, how it is different (and better than) Windows, how to use and navigate it on your machine, and terminal commands that have a wide variety of practical uses.


So what are you waiting for!?!? Go learn some Linux! And say hi to our Mascot, Detective Pingo, along the way.
